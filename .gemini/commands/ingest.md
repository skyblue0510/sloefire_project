# Command: /ingest
## Purpose
Initialize the "Ingestion" phase of the consulting cycle.

## Workflow
1. **Trend Scanning**: Invoke `Trend Translator` to analyze the latest AI reports relevant to the customer's field.
2. **Context Retrieval**: Read the latest `Mastery Evaluation Report` from `shared-memory/client/current/mastery_report.md`.
3. **Theme Definition**: Synthesize a "Month's Theme" that balances market opportunities with the customer's current growth needs.
4. **Output**: Present the theme and 3 key "Profit Opportunity Points" to the customer for approval.
